## Itinerary

**Note:** All times are variable and adjust to cruise ship arrival/departure times.

1. **Pickup - Busan International Passenger Terminal**
   - 부산항국제여객터미널 크루즈탑승장

   - Pickup time adjusts to cruise ship arrival (typically 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM)

2. **Yonggungsa Temple** (1 hour)
   - Beautiful seaside temple with ocean views
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

3. **United Nations Memorial Cemetery** (1 hour)
   - Memorial cemetery for UN soldiers from Korean War
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

4. **Songdo Beach** (1.5 hours)
   - Busan's first public beach
   - Optional: Songdo Sky Cable Car (pay on-site)
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

5. **Lunch at Local Restaurant** (1 hour)
   - Authentic Korean dishes
   - Optional, Extra fee
   - Dietary restrictions should be informed in advance

6. **Gamcheon Culture Village** (1.5 hours)
   - Colorful hillside village, "Machu Picchu of Busan"
   - Vibrant painted houses and art installations
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

7. **Yongdusan Park**
   - Park in the heart of Busan
   - Panoramic city views
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

8. **Nampo-dong**
   - Bustling shopping and cultural district
   - Traditional markets
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

9. **Jagalchi Market**
   - Korea's largest seafood market
   - Fresh seafood and local vendors
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

10. **Drop-off - Busan International Passenger Terminal**
    - Return to cruise terminal
    - 100% guaranteed return before cruise departure

## Steps to Add

1. Open your Supabase project
2. Go to SQL Editor
3. Copy the contents of `supabase/insert-busan-cruise-sightseeing-tour.sql`
4. Paste into the SQL Editor
5. Click "Run" or press `Ctrl+Enter`
6. Verify the tour was created successfully
