## Two Itineraries

The tour offers two different itineraries depending on which port the cruise ship docks at:

### Itinerary A: For Jeju Port Departures

1. **Seongsan Ilchulbong** (2 hours)
   - UNESCO World Natural Heritage site
   - Iconic volcanic tuff cone
   - Sightseeing

2. **Seopjikoji** (1.5 hours)
   - Beautiful coastal area with scenic views
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing, Walk

3. **Seongeup Folk Village** (1 hour)
   - Well-preserved traditional village
   - Guided tour, Walk

4. **Dongmun Traditional Market** (1 hour)
   - Vibrant local market
   - Free time

### Itinerary B: For Seogwipo Gangjeong Port Departures

1. **Hallasan National Park** (1.5 hours)
   - South Korea's highest mountain
   - UNESCO World Heritage Site
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing, Walk

2. **Cheonjiyeon Waterfall** (1.5 hours)
   - "The Pond of the Gods"
   - Three graceful tiers
   - Sightseeing

3. **Jusangjeollidae** (1.5 hours)
   - Dramatic hexagonal lava cliffs
   - Natural Monument No. 443
   - Sightseeing

4. **Seogwipo Olle Market** (1.5 hours)
   - Local market with fresh produce and local specialties
   - Sightseeing

## Important Information

### Know Before You Go

- **Contact Information:** Please let us know the name of your cruise ship and your arrival time. Also, make sure to provide a WhatsApp number where we can reach you. You can get a faster response using WhatsApp.
- **Pickup Times:** Pickup and drop-off times are scheduled to match the cruise ship's arrival and departure times (06:00~22:00). You will be picked up when your cruise arrives at the port (can be changed at any time).
- **Tour Flexibility:** Tour time may change depending on cruise arrival and departure times. Tour schedule may be reduced or changed on site due to limited time. The tour duration and number of attractions can be adjusted based on the cruise's stay in Jeju.
- **Two Ports:** There are two ports in Jeju Island: Jeju Port and Gangjeong Seogwipo Port. Make sure to select the correct one when booking.
- **Lunch:** Lunch is available at local restaurants (at your own expense). Please inform us in advance about any dietary restrictions.

### Private Car Option

- For Private Car option: You can customize the day and destinations
- Entrance fees are not included in the private car option

## Steps to Add

1. Open your Supabase project
2. Go to SQL Editor
3. Copy the contents of `supabase/insert-jeju-cruise-full-day-tour.sql`
4. Paste into the SQL Editor
5. Click "Run" or press `Ctrl+Enter`
6. Verify the tour was created successfully

## Verification

After running the SQL script, you should see:
- Tour created with ID notification
- Verification query showing tour details
- 2 pickup points listed (Seogwipo Gangjeong Cruise Terminal and Port of Jeju)
