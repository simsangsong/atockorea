## 500자 버전 (상세 입력칸용)

ATOC KOREA LLC operates an online travel booking and reservation platform.
We act solely as an intermediary between customers and independent third-party tour operators.

Our services include displaying tour listings, facilitating reservations, processing online payments for booking services, and issuing booking confirmations.

We do not operate tours, employ guides or drivers, or provide on-site travel services.
All tours and experiences are delivered by independent providers, who are responsible for tour execution, safety, and compliance with applicable laws.
