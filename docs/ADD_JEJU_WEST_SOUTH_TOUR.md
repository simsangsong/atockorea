## Itinerary

1. **Pickup** (08:30-09:05): 4 pickup locations
   - Ocean Suites Jeju (08:30)
   - Jeju Airport 3rd Floor, Gate 3 (08:45)
   - Lotte City Hotel Jeju (08:55)
   - Shilla Duty-Free Jeju Store (09:05)

2. **Mt. Halla - Eoseungsaengak Trail** (09:30-11:00)
   - Easy trail with beautiful views of Hallasan
   - Weather-dependent: may change to 1100 Altitude Wetland or Saebyeol Oreum
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

3. **O'sulloc Tea Museum & Green Tea Fields** (11:30-12:30)
   - Explore Jeju's most famous tea museum
   - Stroll through wide green tea fields
   - Enjoy green tea desserts
   - Free time, Sightseeing

4. **Lunch at Local Restaurant** (13:00-14:00)
   - Authentic Korean dishes
   - Optional, Extra fee
   - All dietary needs accommodated

5. **Jusangjeollidae (Jusangjeolli Cliff)** (14:30-15:30)
   - Dramatic volcanic rock pillars
   - Geometric patterns formed by lava flows
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

6. **Hueree Nature Life Park** (16:00-17:00)
   - Peaceful nature park
   - Seasonal flowers and countryside charm
   - Optional: Jeju Black Pig Feeding Experience (KRW 2,000 on-site)
   - Guided tour, Sightseeing

7. **Tangerine Picking Experience** (17:00-17:30)
   - Visit a nearby orchard
   - Pick fresh Jeju tangerines by hand
   - Pay on-site

8. **Jeongbang Waterfall** (17:30-18:00)
   - Rare coastal waterfall dropping into the sea
   - If closed due to weather, replaced with Cheonjiyeon Waterfall
   - Free time, Sightseeing

9. **Drop-off** (18:00+)
   - Shilla Duty Free Jeju
   - LOTTE City Hotel Jeju
   - Jeju Airport (CJU)
   - Ocean Suites Jeju Hotel
   - Jeju Dongmun Traditional Market (Self-Guided, Only drop)
